What This Holds

Carbon

The evidence story ties atmospheric warming to soil carbon loss and the case for biological drawdown.

Economics

Farm resilience, cost pressure, and profitability are treated as part of the same system as soil health.

Water

Water security is framed as a soil-and-climate problem, not a disconnected resource problem.
