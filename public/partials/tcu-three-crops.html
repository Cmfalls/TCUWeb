Three Crops. Three Proofs. One Model.

Each crop was chosen because it represents a real and distinct challenge facing American agriculture.

Together, these three programs form a single coherent proof. Regenerative agriculture works not just for one crop or one farm type, but across the conditions that define American agriculture. What we prove at Edison can apply across millions of acres.

→ The Climate Stress Test
