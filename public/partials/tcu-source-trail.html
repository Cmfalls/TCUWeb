01 / CLAIM FOOD
31%
Food systems account for roughly 31% of anthropogenic greenhouse-gas emissions.
Crippa et al., Nature Food, 2021; IPCC AR6 WGIII food-system context
TCU Stats food-system synthesis
Reviewed April 2026

Why this number matters

The 31% figure places food and agriculture squarely inside climate strategy rather than alongside it. When food is this large a share of emissions, regenerative agriculture stops being a niche topic and becomes a core lever.

03 / CLAIM CARBON
5.8 TONS C

Why this number matters

This is the physical quantity of carbon actually moved out of the atmosphere and into biological storage on one acre. It's the direct climate measurement, before any conversion.
